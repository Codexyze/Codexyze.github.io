BiteBuddy Privacy Policy

At BiteBuddy, your privacy matters to us. This page explains how we handle your data while you use our app.

1. Data Collection

BiteBuddy does not collect personal information. The app uses the internet only to load ads, and these ads are loaded only when you choose to click on them.

2. Advertisements

Ads in BiteBuddy are shown only when you tap a button that clearly states it is a feedback ad. We never force ads or display them without your consent.

3. Accuracy of Data

The food dataset used in the app may not be 100% accurate but is suitable for general tracking and educational purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our ads are served via trusted third-party ad networks. We do not share or sell your data to any third party.

5. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here for full transparency.

Summary: We respect your privacy. Ads are only shown when you explicitly click for them. No data is collected or shared. Food data is general-purpose and may have slight inaccuracies.
