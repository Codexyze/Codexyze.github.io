BiteBuddy Privacy Policy

At BiteBuddy, your privacy matters to us. This page explains how we handle your data while you use our app.

1. Data Collection

BiteBuddy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. All your app usage data (like meals, workouts, and logs) stays locally on your device. The app only connects to the internet to display banner or optional ads.

2. Advertisements

BiteBuddy displays banner ads and optional reward/feedback ads served via trusted ad networks such as Google AdMob. These ads may use anonymous identifiers (like device ID) for ad personalization and frequency capping as per Google’s standard ad-serving process.

You can choose to opt out of personalized ads anytime through your Android settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

We never force ads during core app usage — ads appear only in clearly designated spaces (like the bottom banner area or when you voluntarily tap the “Support via Ad” button).

3. Accuracy of Data

The food and nutrition dataset used in BiteBuddy is self-created and intended for general health awareness and educational purposes. Nutritional values may not be 100% accurate for medical or clinical use.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party ad providers (like Google AdMob) for serving ads. No personal information or health data is shared with these services. BiteBuddy does not use analytics, tracking, or external databases.

5. Changes to Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect feature or compliance changes. Updates will always be posted here for full transparency.

Summary: BiteBuddy is an offline, privacy-first app. Ads are limited to banners or voluntary views only. No personal or health data is collected, stored, or shared. Food data is general-purpose and may have small inaccuracies.
