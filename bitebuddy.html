BiteBuddy Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 3, 2025

At BiteBuddy, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data while you use our app.

By using BiteBuddy, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

1. Data Collection & Storage

BiteBuddy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data to external servers. All your app usage data stays locally on your device, including:

  • Food consumption logs and nutrition tracking
  • Exercise and workout records
  • Water intake logs
  • Menstrual cycle tracking data
  • Progress photos (stored locally on your device)
  • Personal notes and health metrics

Your data never leaves your device and is completely under your control.

2. Device Storage Access

BiteBuddy requests access to your device storage (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES permission) only for the Progress Tracking feature. This permission allows you to:

  • Select and save progress photos from your device gallery
  • Track your fitness/health journey visually

These images are stored locally and never uploaded. You can revoke this permission anytime.

3. Internet Usage

BiteBuddy uses the internet only for advertisements and Firebase in-app messaging. No personal health data (food logs, exercise records, menstrual data, or any other information) is transmitted to our servers.

4. Advertisements

BiteBuddy displays ads via Google AdMob. These ads may use anonymous device identifiers for personalization and frequency control.

  • Banner ads only
  • Optional rewarded ads
  • No forced full-screen interruptions

5. Firebase In-App Messaging

BiteBuddy uses Firebase In-App Messaging to display occasional updates, tips, or feature announcements.

This service may collect anonymous interaction data (such as app usage signals) to improve message relevance. No personal health data is shared.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase In-App Messaging

These services may collect limited anonymous data according to their own privacy policies. BiteBuddy does not share any personal health data with them.

7. Data Security

All user data is stored locally on your device using secure storage. BiteBuddy has no access to your data.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device and is retained only until you choose to delete it.

  • Delete individual logs inside the app
  • Clear app data from device settings
  • Uninstall the app (removes all data permanently)

Once deleted, data cannot be recovered. BiteBuddy does not store backups.

9. Children's Privacy

BiteBuddy is intended for users aged 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when necessary.
