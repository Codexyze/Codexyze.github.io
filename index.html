👨‍💻 Behind the App

Built by an Android Developer Who Loves Learning

Audio Cutter was built as a deep exploration into Android's native media ecosystem. Instead of relying on external media engines, the app leverages Media3 Transformer, ExoPlayer, Jetpack Compose and Clean Architecture to provide powerful media editing directly on device.
