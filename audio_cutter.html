Privacy Policy

Audio & Video Cutter • Transparency • Your Data, Your Control

Effective Date: July 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Audio & Video Cutter (referred to as “the App”) handles information and user data. We believe privacy should be simple and transparent, especially for tools that work directly with your personal media.

The App is built as an offline-first media editing application. We do not operate backend servers, and we do not collect or store personal data ourselves.

Media Access & App Functionality

Audio & Video Cutter accesses media files stored on your device only when you explicitly select them. This access is required to provide the following features:

  • Audio trimming and ringtone creation
  • Video trimming for short clips and status videos
  • Extracting audio from video files
  • Merging multiple audio files into a single track

All processing — including trimming, extracting, merging, and converting — happens entirely on your device. Your audio and video files are never uploaded or shared.

Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share personal information such as:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Edited or original audio and video content
  • Any personally identifiable information

Your files remain under your full control and stay on your device at all times.

Permissions

The App requests permissions strictly required for its core features:

  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO – Access audio files (Android 13+)
  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO – Access video files (Android 13+)
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Media access on Android 12 and below
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Save processed media files

These permissions are used only when you choose files to edit or save.

Advertising & Third-Party Services

The App displays advertisements using Google AdMob, including banner and interstitial ads.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information to serve ads and measure performance. This data is collected by Google, not by the App developer.

Information AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (or a non-personalized alternative)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions and clicks)
  • Crash and performance diagnostics

The advertising ID is used only for ad delivery and measurement. It is not used for personal identification by us.

You can manage ad personalization through your Google account and device settings. Learn more here: Google Privacy Policy

Internet Usage

Internet access is required only to load and display advertisements. All media editing features work fully offline.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect new features or legal requirements. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
