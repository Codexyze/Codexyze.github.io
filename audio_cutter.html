Privacy Policy

Audio & Video Cutter • Transparency • Your Data, Your Control

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Audio & Video Cutter (referred to as "the App") handles information and user data. We believe privacy should be simple and transparent, especially for tools that work directly with your personal media.

The App is built as an offline-first media editing application. All media processing happens on your device. We do not operate our own backend servers for media processing, though some features integrate with trusted third-party services (see Third-Party Services below).

Core Features & On-Device Processing

Audio & Video Cutter accesses media files and device settings only when you explicitly initiate an action. All media processing happens entirely on your device:

  • Audio trimming and ringtone creation
  • Video trimming for short clips and status videos
  • Extracting audio from video files
  • Merging multiple audio files into a single track
  • Recording audio directly using your device microphone
  • Converting between audio and video formats

Your audio and video files are never uploaded to any server. All editing, trimming, merging, extracting, converting, and recording happens locally on your device.

Permissions Explained

The App requests permissions strictly required for its features:

Media & File Access

  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO – Read audio files from your device (Android 13+)
  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO – Read video files from your device (Android 13+)
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Media access on Android 12 and below
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Save processed audio and video files

Recording & Audio

  • RECORD_AUDIO – Record audio using your device microphone (only when you start recording)

Ringtone & System Settings

  • WRITE_SETTINGS – Set edited audio as a ringtone or alarm tone on your device

Notifications

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Send notifications (e.g., processing updates, subscription reminders)
What you control: You can revoke any permission anytime in your device Settings. Revoking a permission simply disables that feature in the App.

Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share personal information such as:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Original or edited audio and video content
  • Recorded microphone audio
  • Location data
  • Browsing or app usage history

Your files remain under your full control and stay on your device at all times.

Local Data Storage

The App stores limited data locally on your device to improve your experience:

  • Recent History: Paths to recently edited files (stored locally for quick access)
  • App Settings: Your preferences (playback speed, default export format, etc.)
  • Subscription Status: Local cache of your Pro subscription status for offline use

This data is stored only on your device and is never uploaded or shared. You can clear this data anytime by uninstalling the App or clearing App data in Settings.

Delete local data: Go to Settings > Apps > Audio & Video Cutter > Storage > Clear Data

Third-Party Services

While media processing happens on your device, the App integrates with the following trusted third-party services:

1. Google AdMob (Advertising)

The App displays advertisements using Google AdMob (banner and interstitial ads). AdMob may automatically collect certain data to serve and measure ads. This data is collected by Google, not by us.

Information AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (or non-personalized alternative)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions and clicks)
  • Crash and performance diagnostics

You can manage ad personalization through your Google account settings. Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

2. RevenueCat & Google Play Billing (Subscriptions)

The App offers a Pro subscription for advanced features. Subscription processing is handled through:

  • Google Play Billing: Processes payment transactions securely
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription status and entitlements

RevenueCat may collect:

  • Subscription status and plan information
  • Purchase history (managed by Google Play)
  • Device identifiers (for license verification)
  • Analytics about subscription usage

Payment details are processed securely by Google Play and are never shared with us or RevenueCat. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through Google Play > My Apps > Audio & Video Cutter > Subscription.

Learn more: RevenueCat Privacy Policy • Google Play Privacy

3. Firebase Cloud Messaging (Push Notifications)

The App uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications. This service is provided by Google and may collect:

  • FCM registration tokens (for delivery identification)
  • Device identifiers
  • Notification interaction data (delivered, opened, dismissed)

What notifications are used for:

  • Processing updates (e.g., "Your audio is ready")
  • Feature announcements and updates
  • Subscription reminders
  • Important app notifications

You can disable notifications anytime:

  • App Level: Go to the App's notification settings and toggle off
  • System Level: Go to Settings > Apps > Audio & Video Cutter > Notifications > Toggle Off

Learn more: Firebase Privacy & Security

Internet Usage

Internet access is required only for:

  • Loading and displaying advertisements (AdMob)
  • Processing subscription and entitlement checks (RevenueCat)
  • Receiving push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

All media editing, recording, and file conversion features work fully offline. You can use the App without an internet connection for core media editing.

Your Controls & Data Management

You have full control over your data and how the App uses your device:

Revoke Permissions

  • Go to Settings > Apps > Audio & Video Cutter > Permissions
  • Toggle off any permission you don't want to grant

Disable Notifications

  • Go to Settings > Apps > Audio & Video Cutter > Notifications > Toggle Off
  • Or disable within the App's settings

Manage Your Subscription

  • Open Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions
  • Find "Audio & Video Cutter" and tap to modify or cancel

Delete Local Data

  • Go to Settings > Apps > Audio & Video Cutter > Storage > Clear Data
  • This removes recent history, settings, and cached subscription info

Opt Out of Personalized Ads

  • Go to Settings > Google > Manage your Google Account > Data & Privacy
  • Find "Ad Personalization" and toggle off
Note: Revoking permissions or deleting data may limit app functionality, but you remain in full control.

Data Security & Accuracy

While the App processes all media on your device (which is secure by design), we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to third-party services (AdMob, RevenueCat, Firebase). We use industry-standard practices and partner with trusted providers, but network transmission inherently carries minimal risk.

For maximum privacy, you can disable internet features (ads, subscriptions, notifications) and use the App for offline media editing.
